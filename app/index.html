I am a Full Stack Web Developer and Anthropologist who makes applications that are engaging and accessible.
I work to create user-friendly, effective applications that take in user input to create the best program possible. In today's world, I see technology as the best way to reach a broad audience and in order to reach those audiences, applications need to be accessible to anyone and grab the user’s interest.
My academic background as an anthropologist has inspired my interest in the way that information is presented to the public and the way people interact with technology. I take these things into account in the way that I design both the front-end and back-end aspects of my web applications.
